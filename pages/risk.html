Risk Management,

At Razor Markets, we understand that trading financial instruments, especially Contracts for Difference (CFDs), carries inherent risks. While market opportunities can be rewarding, it is crucial to approach trading with a well-defined risk management strategy. Our goal is to empower traders with the knowledge, tools, and support needed to navigate market volatility responsibly.

Understanding Trading Risks

Trading CFDs involves speculation on price movements without owning the underlying asset. This means:

Leverage Amplifies Risk

While leverage can increase potential profits, it also magnifies losses.

Market Volatility

Rapid price fluctuations can result in unexpected losses.

Liquidity Risks

Some assets may have lower liquidity, leading to price gaps and potential slippage.

Emotional Trading

Fear and greed can impact decision-making, leading to impulsive trades.

How Razor Markets Helps You Manage Risk

We prioritize risk awareness and responsible trading by offering:

Risk Management Tools

  • Stop-Loss & Take-Profit Orders – Automatically close positions at predefined price levels to minimize potential losses and secure profits.
  • Negative Balance Protection – Ensures that your losses do not exceed your account balance.
  • Margin Call Alerts – Notifications when your margin level is at risk, helping you take corrective action in time.

Forex Trading
